## Chat 1

| Japanese                       | Romaji                           | English                      |
| ------------------------------ | -------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| いらっしゃいませ               | Irasshaimase                     | Welcome                      |
| すみません、これはいくらですか | Sumimasen, kore wa ikura desu ka | Excuse me, how much is this? |
| ひとつ 100 円です              | Hitotsu hyaku en desu            | One is 100 yen               |
| じゃ、それを三つください       | Ja, sore o mittsu kudasai        | Then, I'll take three please |
| 全部でいくらですか             | Zenbu de ikura desu ka           | How much is it in total?     |
| 300 円です                     | Sanbyaku en desu                 | It's 300 yen                 |

## Chat 2

| Japanese                                 | Romaji                                          | English                                       |
| ---------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| いらっしゃいませ                         | Irasshaimase                                    | Welcome                                       |
| シャツを見せてください                   | Shatsu o misete kudasai                         | Please show me the shirts                     |
| はい、どうぞ                             | Hai, dōzo                                       | Here you are                                  |
| いくらですか                             | Ikura desu ka                                   | How much is it?                               |
| 一万円です                               | Ichiman en desu                                 | It's 10,000 yen                               |
| 高いですね。もっと安いシャツはありますか | Takai desu ne. Motto yasui shatsu wa arimasu ka | That's expensive. Do you have cheaper shirts? |
| はい、どうぞ。五千円です                 | Hai, dōzo. Gosen en desu                        | Yes, here you go. It's 5,000 yen              |
| そうですか。じゃ、これをください         | Sō desu ka. Ja, kore o kudasai                  | I see. Then, I'll take this one please        |
